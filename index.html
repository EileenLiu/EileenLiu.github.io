Epic's Epic Epic
Development Ongoing.

Flash game with one month development cycle in a group of 5.

Epic's Epic Epic is a 2D hack-and-slash platformer that emphasizes player exploration. Our hero’s name is Epic. He lives peacefully in his little village, but little does he know that his “world” is actually a monster, which lives in a world on top of a monster, with monsters all the way down. Embark on an epic journey across miniature worlds inside other worlds, controlling larger and larger monsters.













Download Pirates of the Stratosphere

Pirates of the Stratosphere Released May 2013.

A side scrolling shooter game developed with C# and XNA in a group of 5.

Winner of Cornell University GDIAC Audience Favorite Award (Showcase 2013, Intro Division). Featured in BOOM (Bits On Our Minds) 2014.

Legend speaks of an era called the Age of Aeronautic Exploration, a time long ago when ships first took to the clouds. Many believe it to be myth, as history prior to it has been lost, no longer a part of the memory of mankind. People have lived all their lives on airships, just as those generations and generations before them had.

Take to the skies in this highflying, side-scrolling pirate adventure filled with ship combat, commandeering, and plundering. You are Fausto, the boldest sky pirate captain around and you will stop at nothing to get more booty. Every ship is fair game when you’re running low on resources, and the only way you can continue is to risk it all and jump ship.

  • Fight through intense bullet-hell ship-to-ship combat
  • Commandeer variety of enemies to steal their ships
  • Explore beautifully rugged pirate worlds

Escape from Clockworktopia
Released September 2013.

Created for a 24 hour game jam for the prompt "Gears".

Escape from Clockworktopia was made using Java and libgdx in a group of 3. The player is a steampunk tank escaping from in a procedurally generated clockwork machine. Use W and S to move up and down ramps between several layers of platforms while dodging gears falling from above. Use mouse to shoot projectiles and destroy falling gears.

Download CritterWorld

CritterWorld
Released December 2012.

A top down world simulation developed in java with a partner. In CritterWorld, Critters move, eat, grow, fight, and breed while plants grow and bud. The ancestry of each critter can be viewed and occasionally offspring will have mutations displayed in their coloring. The user controls critter/plant actions and placement through the user interface. Critter behavior and world layout can also be specified through imported text files. Final project for CS2112 - Honors Object-Oriented Design and Data Structures.

Masquerade
Released June 2011.

A character creation and dress up game with over 16963309440 possible character appearance combinations and a detailed user interface. The player can customize the appearance and costume of their character by selecting different costume items, facial features, and backgrounds. The latest changed item can be changed to a different color with color sliders in different color modes. Character background and personality can be filled out in the description form by typing or selecting suggested traits. Developed in java as a solo project for AP Computer Science at TJHSST.
